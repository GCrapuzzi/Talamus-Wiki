# AI Product Milestone Planning

## Summary

A structured, iterative process for setting and adjusting AI product goals by first evaluating the capabilities of existing off-the-shelf foundation models, thereby grounding expectations in current technical reality.

## Core Idea

AI product goals are not static. The planning process must start with a thorough evaluation of existing model capabilities to accurately estimate the required effort and potential return, preventing over-ambitious initial goals.

## Practical Use

1. **Evaluate:** Benchmark current off-the-shelf models against the target use case. 2. **Estimate:** Determine the gap between current capability and the desired goal. 3. **Adjust:** Be prepared to adjust the goal (scope reduction) if the required resources exceed the potential return on investment (ROI).
