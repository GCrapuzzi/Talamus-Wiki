# Competitive Advantage Triad

## Summary

A model for identifying three primary sources of competitive advantage in AI products: Technology, Data, and Distribution.

## Core Idea

In the AI landscape, defensibility is rarely based on a single factor. Understanding which of the three pillars (Technology, Data, Distribution) is most difficult for large incumbents to replicate is crucial for strategic planning.

## Practical Use

When evaluating a market opportunity, map the potential competitive advantage. If large companies dominate distribution (e.g., Google Docs), the startup must focus on building a unique data advantage or a niche technology that is hard to replicate.
