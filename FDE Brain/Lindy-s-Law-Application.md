# Lindy's Law Application

## Summary

The principle that the future life expectancy of a technology or concept is proportional to its current age. The longer something has existed, the longer it is expected to continue existing.

## Core Idea

It serves as a heuristic for assessing the stability and persistence of technological trends or problem domains. If a concept has survived multiple cycles, it is likely to remain relevant.

## Practical Use

When evaluating the long-term viability of an AI solution or market niche, consult Lindy's Law. If a core concept (e.g., structured data, specific ML paradigm) has been around for decades, it suggests a stable foundation for development.
