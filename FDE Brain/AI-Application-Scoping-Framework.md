# AI Application Scoping Framework

## Summary

A critical planning framework emphasizing the need to evaluate and prioritize potential AI applications before committing to development, preventing resource waste and scope creep.

## Core Idea

Given the seemingly limitless potential of AI, this framework mandates a disciplined approach to ensure that development effort is focused on high-impact, necessary applications, rather than merely 'fun' projects.

## Practical Use

Applying this framework during the initial discovery phase of any AI project to define clear boundaries, measure potential ROI, and establish minimum viable product (MVP) goals.
