# AI Tutoring/Scaffolding

## Summary

Utilizing AI not as a solution provider, but as a dynamic tutor that guides the user through the learning process, helping them build foundational knowledge and eventually surpass the AI's current capabilities.

## Core Idea

The risk of AI replacing skills is offset by the opportunity to use AI as a highly accessible, personalized tutor. The goal is to use AI to accelerate initial understanding, enabling the human to continue learning and achieving mastery independently.

## Practical Use

Design a multi-stage learning application where the AI initially provides structured guidance (scaffolding) and gradually reduces its assistance (fading) as the user demonstrates competence, forcing independent problem-solving.
