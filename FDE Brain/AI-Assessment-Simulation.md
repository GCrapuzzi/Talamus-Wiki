# AI Assessment & Simulation

## Summary

Employing LLMs to generate diverse assessment materials (quizzes, open-ended questions) and simulating complex scenarios (e.g., debate, roleplay) for practice and evaluation.

## Core Idea

AI excels at generating varied, high-volume practice content and maintaining multiple viewpoints, making it superior to human-generated content for comprehensive practice and testing.

## Practical Use

Build modules that allow users to engage in role-playing scenarios (e.g., language practice) or debate topics with the AI. Implement automated grading pipelines for both multiple-choice and open-ended submissions.
