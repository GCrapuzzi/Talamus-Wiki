# General-Purpose AI Capabilities

## Summary

The ability of Foundation Models to perform a vast array of tasks—many previously considered impossible or impractical—leading to exponential growth in potential applications.

## Core Idea

FMs are powerful not just because they improve existing tasks, but because they enable entirely new categories of applications (e.g., generating high-quality media, synthesizing training data, writing complex code).

## Practical Use

When brainstorming use cases, focus on the *potential* rather than the current limitations. Identify tasks that are currently manual, communication-heavy, or require complex media generation, as these are prime candidates for FM automation.
