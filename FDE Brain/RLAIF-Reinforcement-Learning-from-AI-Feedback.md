# RLAIF (Reinforcement Learning from AI Feedback)

## Summary

A preference finetuning technique where the alignment signal (reward) is generated by a sophisticated AI model rather than human annotators.

## Core Idea

RLAIF scales the alignment process by leveraging automated feedback loops. This is crucial for large-scale deployment where human labeling is prohibitively expensive or slow, allowing for consistent, high-volume feedback.

## Practical Use

Useful for maintaining alignment across massive datasets or in domains where human consensus is difficult to achieve. Potentially used by models like Claude.
