# Internal vs. External AI Deployment Strategy

## Summary

A strategic framework recommending that organizations prioritize deploying internal-facing AI applications (e.g., internal knowledge management) over external-facing applications (e.g., customer support chatbots) initially.

## Core Idea

Internal applications minimize risks associated with data privacy, compliance, and potential catastrophic failures, allowing companies to build AI engineering expertise and gain confidence before deploying to public-facing customer interactions.

## Practical Use

When advising a client on their first AI project, recommend starting with internal tools (e.g., summarizing internal documents, automating internal reporting) to establish a low-risk proof of concept and build organizational capability.
