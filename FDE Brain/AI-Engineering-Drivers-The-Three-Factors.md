# AI Engineering Drivers (The Three Factors)

## Summary

Three macro-factors driving the rapid growth and adoption of AI Engineering as a distinct discipline.

## Core Idea

The confluence of general-purpose capabilities, massive investment, and low technical barriers has created ideal conditions for rapid, accessible AI application development.

## Practical Use

When pitching an AI project, frame the opportunity by referencing these three drivers to demonstrate market readiness and feasibility. This helps justify the engineering approach.
