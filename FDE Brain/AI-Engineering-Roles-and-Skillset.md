# AI Engineering Roles and Skillset

## Summary

Defines the cross-functional technical roles required to build, optimize, and manage AI applications in a corporate setting.

## Core Idea

AI engineering is not a single role. It requires collaboration between specialized technical roles (AI Engineers, ML Engineers, Data Scientists) and strategic roles (Technical Product Managers, Engineering Managers) to ensure both technical feasibility and business alignment.

## Practical Use

When staffing a project, use this glossary to identify skill gaps. If the team lacks 'technical product management' skills, the project risks building a technically sound but commercially unviable product.
