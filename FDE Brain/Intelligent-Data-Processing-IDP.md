# Intelligent Data Processing (IDP)

## Summary

The process of automatically extracting structured, usable information from unstructured sources such as contracts, receipts, reports, and documents.

## Core Idea

IDP transforms qualitative, messy data into quantitative, searchable data, making previously inaccessible information actionable for downstream AI models and business processes.

## Practical Use

Building pipelines to ingest enterprise documents (e.g., invoices, legal contracts) and automatically populate structured databases or fields, eliminating manual data entry.
