# Generative AI Workflow for Creative Assets

## Summary

A structured process for leveraging generative models (text-to-image, text-to-video) to rapidly prototype, iterate, and localize high-volume marketing and advertising content.

## Core Idea

AI's probabilistic nature makes it ideal for creative tasks, allowing organizations to move from concept to multiple drafts (e.g., multiple ad variations, seasonal changes) at scale, significantly reducing the time spent on initial ideation and first drafts. Human expertise shifts from creation to curation and refinement.

## Practical Use

1. **A/B Testing:** Generate dozens of ad variations (visuals, themes) based on defined parameters (e.g., product angle, emotional tone) for rapid testing. 2. **Localization/Seasonal Variation:** Programmatically adjust assets (e.g., changing leaf colors for fall, adding snow for winter) to adapt campaigns for different regions or seasons without manual re-rendering. 3. **Drafting:** Use AI to generate initial storyboards, promotional images, or video concepts that human experts can then refine and polish.
