# Regulatory Compliance Planning (AI)

## Summary

A structured approach to identifying, mitigating, and planning for legal and regulatory risks associated with AI deployment (e.g., data sovereignty, IP rights, privacy).

## Core Idea

AI technologies are increasingly viewed as national security issues, making compliance a primary engineering concern. Failure to plan for regulations (like GDPR or evolving IP laws) can halt product development or result in massive fines.

## Practical Use

Integrate legal and compliance experts into the initial design phase. Specifically audit data provenance, model training data sources (for IP risk), and compute resource sourcing (for geopolitical/national security risk).
