# Conversational Agents

## Summary

AI systems designed to interact with users via dialogue (text, voice, or 3D) to provide information, guide users through complex tasks, and simulate interactions.

## Core Idea

Conversational agents serve as versatile, multi-modal interfaces that automate complex workflows and improve user experience by providing immediate, guided assistance.

## Practical Use

Developing customer support bots for immediate issue resolution, or building product copilots that guide users through multi-step, confusing processes like filing insurance claims or managing corporate policies.
