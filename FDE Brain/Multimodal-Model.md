# Multimodal Model

## Summary

A model capable of processing and generating content conditioned on multiple data modalities (e.g., text, images, video, audio, 3D assets) simultaneously.

## Core Idea

Real-world AI requires processing data beyond text. Multimodal models bridge the gap between language processing and other sensory inputs, making AI capable of operating in complex, real-world environments.

## Practical Use

Implement systems that require cross-modal understanding, such as analyzing an image (vision) and generating a descriptive caption (text), or processing a video feed to detect objects and summarize the event.
