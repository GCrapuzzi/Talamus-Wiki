# Information Aggregation & Distillation

## Summary

The operational process of collecting disparate data sources (e.g., meeting notes, emails, Slack conversations) and using AI to synthesize them into concise, actionable insights.

## Core Idea

By efficiently aggregating and distilling information, organizations reduce the cognitive burden on middle management and accelerate decision-making.

## Practical Use

Implementing automated workflows that ingest communication channels (Slack, Teams) and use LLMs to generate structured summaries, identifying key facts, open questions, and assigned action items.
