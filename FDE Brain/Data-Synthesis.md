# Data Synthesis

## Summary

The process of generating artificial data (e.g., text, images, records) to augment, improve, or replace real-world data for AI model training and testing.

## Core Idea

Synthetic data addresses fundamental limitations of real-world datasets, including scarcity, privacy concerns, and class imbalance, allowing for scalable and controllable data supply.

## Practical Use

Used when real data is difficult to obtain (e.g., rare weather events, deep sea exploration) or when the data is highly sensitive and regulated (e.g., patient records).
