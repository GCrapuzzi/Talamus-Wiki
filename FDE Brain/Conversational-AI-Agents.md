# Conversational AI Agents

## Summary

Versatile AI interfaces capable of finding information, explaining concepts, and guiding users through complex tasks across multiple modalities (text, voice, 3D).

## Core Idea

Conversational agents move beyond simple Q&A to act as 'copilots' that guide users through complex, multi-step processes (e.g., filing claims, tax preparation), significantly improving customer experience and reducing operational costs for enterprises.

## Practical Use

Design and implement agents that integrate with backend enterprise systems (CRMs, policy databases) to function as product copilots. Focus on state management and multi-turn dialogue flow to handle complex, confusing tasks that require deep domain knowledge.
