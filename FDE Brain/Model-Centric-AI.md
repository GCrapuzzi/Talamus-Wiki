# Model-Centric AI

## Summary

An AI development paradigm that focuses on improving model performance by enhancing the model itself, such as designing new architectures, increasing model size, or developing novel training techniques.

## Core Idea

Performance improvement is achieved primarily through algorithmic or architectural advancements, assuming the underlying dataset is sufficient.

## Practical Use

Used when the data is known to be robust and comprehensive, and the primary bottleneck is believed to be the model's capacity or efficiency. This approach typically requires significant compute resources.
