# AI Content Misuse (SEO Farming)

## Summary

The practice of using AI to generate massive volumes of low-quality, SEO-optimized content on dedicated websites, primarily to drive traffic and sell advertising space.

## Core Idea

AI's efficiency in generating high-volume, optimized text poses a threat to the integrity of online information, leading to content farms that prioritize ad revenue over informational value.

## Practical Use

When developing content moderation or information retrieval systems, implement filters and classifiers specifically trained to detect patterns associated with high-volume, low-perplexity, or overly optimized AI-generated spam content.
