# AI Last Mile Challenge

## Summary

The significant and often underestimated gap in time, complexity, and effort required to transition a successful, functional initial demonstration (demo) built on foundation models into a robust, scalable, and reliable production-grade product.

## Core Idea

Initial success with impressive foundation models can create a false sense of readiness. The journey from initial success (e.g., 0 to 60%) is vastly easier and faster than the journey to true maturity (e.g., 60% to 100%).

## Practical Use

When presenting timelines or feasibility studies, explicitly budget for the 'last mile.' Do not equate the time taken to build a proof-of-concept demo with the time required for enterprise-grade testing, integration, and reliability improvements.
