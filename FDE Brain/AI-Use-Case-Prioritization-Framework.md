# AI Use Case Prioritization Framework

## Summary

A framework for evaluating the business necessity of an AI application by categorizing the primary driver into three risk levels: existential threat, profit opportunity, or future-proofing.

## Core Idea

The motivation for building an AI application dictates the required development effort, urgency, and whether the solution must be proprietary (in-house) or can be purchased (buy option).

## Practical Use

When starting a new AI project, first determine the business driver: 1) Is the business threatened by AI (highest priority, requires proprietary solution)? 2) Is the goal to boost profits/productivity (medium priority, allows for COTS/buy options)? 3) Is it exploratory/future-proofing (lowest priority, suitable for R&D)?
