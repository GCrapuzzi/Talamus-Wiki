# AI Agents and Tool Use

## Summary

AI systems designed not just to answer questions, but to plan, execute multi-step tasks, and interact with external tools (APIs, search engines, calendars) to achieve a defined goal.

## Core Idea

Agents elevate AI from a passive information source to an active, goal-oriented executor, significantly increasing productivity and economic value by bridging the gap between LLM reasoning and real-world system interaction.

## Practical Use

Developing automated assistants that can book appointments (using calendar APIs), research information (using search APIs), and complete complex, multi-step business workflows without human intervention.
