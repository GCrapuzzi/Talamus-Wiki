# Workflow Automation

## Summary

The use of AI to automate repetitive, multi-step business processes, ranging from simple daily tasks to complex enterprise operations.

## Core Idea

Automation minimizes human effort and operational latency in routine tasks, allowing human capital to focus on strategic, non-repetitive work.

## Practical Use

Implementing automated workflows for lead management, processing invoices, managing customer requests, or handling data entry across multiple enterprise systems.
