# Foundation Models

## Summary

Massively scaled, general-purpose AI models trained on vast datasets, capable of serving as a foundational layer for a wide variety of downstream, specialized applications.

## Core Idea

Their sheer scale and general capabilities allow them to perform diverse tasks (text generation, summarization, coding) without needing to be retrained for every specific use case, making them the core engine of modern AI applications.

## Practical Use

When selecting a model, assess its general capability (Foundation Model) versus the specific task requirements. Use techniques like Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to ground the general model in proprietary, specific data.
