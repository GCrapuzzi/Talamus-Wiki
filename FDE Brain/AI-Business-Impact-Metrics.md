# AI Business Impact Metrics

## Summary

A structured approach to defining success for an AI application by focusing on quantifiable business outcomes rather than purely technical performance metrics.

## Core Idea

The most important metric for an AI project is its impact on the business. Success must be defined by measurable operational improvements (e.g., efficiency, cost reduction, speed) to ensure the AI solution provides tangible ROI.

## Practical Use

Before development, define specific Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) that relate directly to business goals. For a customer support chatbot, metrics should include: 1) Automation percentage of messages, 2) Message volume capacity increase, 3) Response time reduction, and 4) Human labor hours saved.
