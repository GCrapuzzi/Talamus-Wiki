# AI Task Exposure Measurement

## Summary

A metric used to quantify how susceptible a specific task or occupation is to AI automation, defined as the percentage of tasks where AI can reduce the time needed to complete the task by at least 50%.

## Core Idea

Understanding the 'exposure' of a task helps determine the maximum potential value and feasibility of AI intervention. High exposure indicates a ripe area for AI development.

## Practical Use

Before committing resources to an AI solution, apply this concept by analyzing the target job role's workflow. If the task has low exposure, the ROI for an AI solution may be low, requiring a reassessment of the problem definition.
