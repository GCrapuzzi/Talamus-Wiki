# AI Production Readiness Lifecycle

## Summary

The systematic process of moving an AI model or application from a proof-of-concept (PoC) or demonstration phase into a robust, scalable, and reliable production environment.

## Core Idea

AI applications often fail when moving from controlled demo environments to real-world production due to unaddressed issues like scalability, latency, and operational risk. This pattern emphasizes structured optimization beyond mere model accuracy.

## Practical Use

When building an AI application, map out the required steps to ensure the system addresses non-functional requirements (latency, cost, security) alongside functional requirements (accuracy). This guides the implementation of MLOps pipelines.
