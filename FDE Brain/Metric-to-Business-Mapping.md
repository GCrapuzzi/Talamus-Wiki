# Metric-to-Business Mapping

## Summary

A strategic framework that links technical performance metrics (e.g., Factual Consistency score) directly to quantifiable business outcomes (e.g., automation rate, revenue gain).

## Core Idea

AI metrics must be viewed through the lens of the business problem. Understanding this mapping allows engineering teams to prioritize improvements based on their potential ROI, rather than optimizing metrics in isolation.

## Practical Use

Instead of simply reporting 'Factual consistency is 80%,' map it: 'Factual consistency of 80% means we can automate 30% of customer support requests.' This helps determine the 'usefulness threshold'—the minimum score required for the application to provide tangible business value.
