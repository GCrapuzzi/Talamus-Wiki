# AI Engineering vs. ML Engineering

## Summary

AI Engineering is the overarching discipline for building applications using AI models, evolving from ML Engineering. The key shift is the increased involvement of the AI engineer in product decisions and the focus on rapid, end-to-end product integration.

## Core Idea

While ML Engineering focuses heavily on the model development lifecycle (data gathering, training, optimization), AI Engineering encompasses the entire product lifecycle. The availability of foundation models allows AI engineers to be much more involved in the product design and decision-making process, bridging the gap between pure ML research and commercial product deployment.

## Practical Use

Use this distinction to define team roles. If the team is struggling with product integration or user experience, the problem may be scope creep into 'ML Engineering' territory. If the problem is the core model performance, it is an 'ML Engineering' issue. AI Engineering owns the entire stack.
